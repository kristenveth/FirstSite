Images and Links

We are going to discuss how to put images and links on a page. We will discuss several types of links. Also, how to make clickable images.

External Links

There are several types of links. There are internal, external, and local. An external link, goes outside the server, meaning to another website.

  • The A Tag

Local Links

Local links are to another page in your site. It is usually the navigation to direct your to another webpage within your site. The navigation links are usually at the top of the page or down the left side.

Images

Use the img tag or an image. The src attribute allows you to put in the image file. The alt is for accessability.

Links within text.

To do a word as a link, just put the tags before and ater the word. Such as if I wanted to link to TechMoms, I could just put tags in this paragraph.
